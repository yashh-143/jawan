About Jawan Movie

Shah Rukh Khan is back! The superstar is set to star in his first film since 2021's Pathan, and the anticipation is high. Jawan is a highly-anticipated action thriller directed by Atlee, and it promises to be a visual spectacle. The film's trailer was released earlier this month, and it gave fans a glimpse of what to expect. Khan is seen in a never-before-seen avatar, and he looks absolutely menacing. He is also seen in a number of action-packed sequences, and it is clear that this is going to be a high-octane film. In addition to Khan, Jawan also stars Vijay Sethupathi, Nayantara, and Sanya Malhotra. The film is set to be released in Hindi, Tamil, and Telugu on September 7, 2023.

Here are some of the things that fans are most excited about for Jawan: Shah Rukh Khan's return to action: Khan has been away from action films for a few years, so fans are eager to see him back in his element. Atlee's direction: Atlee is known for his stylish and action-packed films, so fans are confident that Jawan will be a visual treat. The supporting cast: Jawan also stars a number of talented actors, including Vijay Sethupathi, Nayantara, and Sanya Malhotra. Jawan is shaping up to be one of the most anticipated films of the year. With a star-studded cast, a talented director, and a high-octane action plot, it is sure to be a hit with audiences. Here are some additional details about the film: The film's budget is estimated to be ₹200–220 crore. The film is being shot in multiple locations, including Mumbai, Spain, and the United Arab Emirates. The film is being produced by Gauri Khan and Atlee under the banners of Red Chillies Entertainment and AGS Entertainment.
